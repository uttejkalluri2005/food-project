About Our Project

What is Food Recipe Generator?

Our innovative platform helps you discover amazing recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. Simply enter your available ingredients, and our smart algorithm will suggest delicious recipes you can create!

How It Works

Using advanced recipe matching algorithms, we cross-reference your ingredients with our extensive database of recipes. The system intelligently suggests recipes that maximize the use of your available ingredients while minimizing waste.

  • Dietary preference filters (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Meal planning and grocery list generation
  • Recipe rating and user reviews
  • Shopping list integration with local stores
